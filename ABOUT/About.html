History of Our Group at University of Education, Winneba

In 2022, we embarked on our academic journey at the University of Education, Winneba, joining the Mathematics Department to pursue a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics Education. From the very beginning, our group was formed, starting as a close-knit team of six members: Eric Ohene Nannor, Patrick Fumey, Musah Atanga, Barnabas Nbamoandor Tegee, Bernard Alorgbey, and Abraham Gbormedoazio.

Later in our first year, during the second semester, we welcomed Charles Adu into our group. Together, we became more than just classmates; we were a family, united by our shared passion for mathematics and technology. We collaborated on numerous projects, which included:


  1. Building an Application: Using Visual Studio, we developed our own software application, enhancing our coding and development skills.


  2. ICT Tools: We engaged with tools like GeoGebra for mathematical modeling and spreadsheets for data analysis.


  3. Video Editing: We explored multimedia tools to produce and edit videos, a creative endeavor that broadened our technological expertise.


  4. Coding and Web Development: Our group mastered JavaScript for programming and HTML for building websites, laying a foundation for future tech endeavors.

Despite being an all-male group, we celebrated diversity in thought and approach, recognizing that our different backgrounds and experiences only strengthened our collaboration. Each member contributed to the group’s success, and together, we created an inclusive atmosphere where everyone’s voice was heard. As we continue our journey, we remain committed to learning and growing as a team, excited for the challenges and opportunities ahead.
